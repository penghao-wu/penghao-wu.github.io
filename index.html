About Me

I am a first-year PhD student at NTU, supervised by Prof. Ziwei Liu. I completed my Master's degree in computer science at UCSD. Before that, I obtained my B.S. degree in Electric and Computer Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2022.

I am interested in computer vision and multi-modal learning.
